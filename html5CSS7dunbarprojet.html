Welcome to the Dunbar Project Website

Dunbar School was completed in January 1918, for the purpose of educating Tucson's African- American students. The school was named after Pual Laurence Dunbar, a renowned African-American children in first througt ninth grades attended Dunbar until 1951, When de jure segregation was eliminated from the school systems of Arizona. When segregation in Arizona was eliminated, Dunbar School became the non-segregated Jhon Spring Junior High school was closed permanently.
